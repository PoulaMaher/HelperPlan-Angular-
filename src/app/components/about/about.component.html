About Us

We Helper Plan, are a team of ethical professionals dedicated to the betterment of the job seekers and domestic workers. Founded in 2016, our online platform is designed to assist the employers as well as the helpers by enabling them to make informed decisions. Our working method is simple and straight; we connect helpers and employers seamlessly, providing both of them a fair recruitment experience.

Gone are the days when the domestic helpers were required to pay unethical, hefty fees to the employment agencies. At Helper Plan, we are committed to providing instant access to both the parties through well structured profiles, screening and a streamlined hiring process.

Why choose us?

Ethical

Helper Plan advocates for fair employment practices by connecting helpers, caregivers, and other job seekers with employers without charging the workers any fees. This approach helps to prevent exploitation and ensure that the workers are not burdened with debt before they start their jobs. We follow ethical practices and look forward to better our system in the days to come.

Responsible

We are highly responsible and equally passionate about what we are doing. Our moral values motivate us to strive even more and work towards the cause of developing a free and fair job market. While there are several agencies that are taking advantage of the candidates, we at Helper Plan are working full length and beyond our capacity to ensure they get access to a fair and equitable hiring process.
