Frequently Asked Questions

HelperPlane is a powerful platform which allows to connect Employers to the best Domestic Helpers, Nannies, Housekeepers or Drivers. We offer the largest jobs opportunities in Hong Kong, Macau, Singapore, Dubai, Doha, Taipei… HelperPlane is not a recruitment agency and we never required any fees to Domestic Helpers or Drivers. We are proud to freely support any Domestic Workers to find a job. We give the freedom to all employers to directly hire a domestic helper without paying any agency fees.

HelperPlane DOESN’T charge any fees to Domestic Employees. We are happy to offer our services to all candidates looking for a job. Our online platform will support you to choose your employers based on your expectations (location, skills, start date…). Why it’s free? We promise transparent hiring and ethical practices.

To easily contact employers, you just need to create your profile on HelperPlane Website or through HelperPlane APP. Please carefully fill in your profile (add a picture, your skills, your experiences…) and you will increase your chances to get a new job.

The best way to get in touch with us is to send an email to our support team. Please carefully describe your problem and we will reply back with a solution! You can also use our internal messaging system (you need to log in to your account).

Need help with your password? Go to your login page on HelperPlane, then click on Lost your Password and Enter the email you use for HelperPlane, and we’ll help you create a new password. Just a few minutes later you will receive an email to activate your new password.

We are sad but we hope to see you later. To cancel your account, you need to Log In and go to your profile. Then, you just have to remove your job offer or your Helper profile.
