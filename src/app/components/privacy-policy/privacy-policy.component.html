Privacy Policy - Helper Plan

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which Helper Plan Limited collects, uses, maintains and discloses information collected from users (each, a “User”). This privacy policy applies to the Helper Plan.com, other Helper Plan-related sites, apps and all products and services offered by Helper Plan.

We respect your privacy and we are committed to protecting your personal data.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information from you when you use our Apps and Website as an Employer:

First Name & Last Name: We collect the first and last names of both employers and candidates as a compulsory measure to personalize your experience on our platform. This personal identity information helps tailor features and interactions to improve usability and satisfaction. Please note that your first and last names are not shared with any third parties without consent and are used solely within service to enhance your personalized app experience. Please note that in case of employer your names will only be shared with other candidates if you choose to connect with them through specific actions within the app.

Social Login (Public Profile & Email from Google/Facebook/Apple): We offer the option for both employers and candidates to use social login features (including public profile and email from Google, Facebook, or Apple) to facilitate a password-less, one-click login process. Collecting this information is optional and solely intended to enhance your convenience by simplifying access to our platform. Social Login data is shared with Google Firebase for technical processing.

Contact List - Email Address: We require both employers and candidates to provide their email addresses to facilitate login and to contact you about service offerings. This compulsory collection of your email addresses ensures we can maintain effective communication. Please note that your email address is not shared with any third parties without your explicit consent.

Contact List – Mobile Number: We collect mobile numbers from both employers and candidates, with the provision of this information being optional. For employers, the mobile number is used solely to offer an alternative method of discussing our services and is not shared with any third parties. For candidates, the mobile number serves not only for discussing services but also enables potential employers and partner agencies to contact you regarding job offers if you choose to publish your resume profile. Please note that sharing your mobile number in this context is tied to the optional creation of your resume profile.

Contact List – WhatsApp Number: We collect WhatsApp numbers from candidates as an optional method to facilitate communication about our services. Additionally, if you opt to publish your resume profile, your WhatsApp number may be shared with potential employers and partner agencies as a contact option for job offers. Please note that sharing your WhatsApp number is contingent upon your choice to create and publish your resume profile, making this an optional sharing feature.

Password: We offer both employers and candidates the option to create a password for authentication purposes. This option is available unless you choose to use social login, which does not require a password. Please be assured that we do not store password in plain text and any password you create is strictly confidential and not shared with any third parties.

City Location: We collect city location data from both employers and candidates only if you choose to provide this information during the creation of job or resume posts. This optional data is used to narrow search results to your specific area, enhancing the relevance of job and resume listings. This information will be published along with the Job Post or Resume profile.

Job Post: We allow employers to optionally provide preferred candidate requirements such as age, gender, religion, nationality, salary offering, and skills for the purpose of publishing job posts on our platform. This enables potential candidates to find and apply for jobs that match their qualifications and interests. Please note that while these job posts are visible to the public on our platform, they are published without linking to the personal identity of the post creator, ensuring your privacy is maintained.

Resume Post: When candidates choose to create a resume profile, they can optionally include various details such as personal summary, work experience, educational background, language skills, other skills, professional qualifications, salary information, gender, nationality, age, and preferred job classifications and locations. These details are collected to enhance your visibility to potential employers who may be looking for candidates with your qualifications and preferences. They use this information to reach out for interviews and hiring processes. While your resume is shared on the platform for all public users to view, please note that your contact details remain confidential. Only verified Employers and Partner agencies can access these, ensuring a layer of privacy and security in your job search.

Non-personal identification information: We collect various types of non-personal identification information from both employers and candidates, including device type (mobile, tablet, desktop), device model, operating system type (such as Windows, Linux, Android, iOS), operating system version, browser type (such as Chrome, Edge, Safari), browser version, web browser cookies, and app crash reports. This information is collected in the background to enrich your user experience through analysis. The collected data is shared with Google Firebase exclusively for analytical purposes and is not used for marketing.

Advertising ID: We collect Advertising IDs from candidates. These IDs are a form of non-personal identification and are utilized solely for the purpose of displaying advertisements through the Google AdMob service.

How We Share Contact List Information

  • When employers create a job post, the job post is made publicly available for candidates and partner agencies to view. We ensure that your personal identity and employer contact list details are not disclosed in the job post and only the employer can choose to contact the candidate.
  • For candidates who have created a Resume Profile, Helper Plan shares the complete profile, including contact details such as email address, phone number, and WhatsApp number, with potential employers and our partner agencies. This facilitates the process of job offers and employment opportunities.
  • For candidates who have not created a Resume Profile, no contact list information is shared. Creating a resume profile is entirely optional and is under the control of the candidate.
  • You can choose not to share your Personal Information with us. However, if you choose not to provide us with the necessary Personal Information, we may not be able to provide you with some or all of our products or services. 
  • We also require your Personal Information to meet our legal obligations. Where the legal basis for the Processing of your Personal Information is to comply with our legal obligations, a failure by you to provide us with certain Personal Information may cause us and/or you to be in breach of these legal obligations. 

Where Do We Send Collected Information:

Collected information is uploaded to https://api2.Helper Plan.com. This URL is Helper Plan's official server and it's being managed under Helper Plan's direct control.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide our products and services, including but not limited to assisting and enabling Candidates in searching and applying for jobs; assisting Employers in recruiting for roles; to process transactions with you; to verify you as a Registered User.
  • To manage and administer our products and services, verify your identity and to ensure the safety and security of our operations.
  • To operate and improve the App and Website.
  • To personalize your experience with the App and Website.
  • To communicate with you about the App, including updates, security alerts, and support inquiries.
  • To analyse trends and usage data to improve the App and Website.
  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To market and promote our products and services. ⁠We use your information to send you marketing and promotional materials for direct marketing purposes including information about our products and features, job placement and related career information, newsletters, survey requests, and events we think may be of interest to you. We may communicate with you via a number of different channels (including email/chat/telephone).
  • To conduct market surveys, trials, and customer research.
  • To send you information regarding the products and services offered by businesses within Helper Plan Group, Advertisers, and Partners.

Deleting Your Personal Data

You have the right to delete your personal data from our systems. To do this, you can follow these steps:

  1. Access Your Profile: Log in to your Helper Plan account and navigate to your profile section.
  2. Delete Account Option: Locate the "Delete Account" option within your profile settings or menu.
  3. Confirm Deletion: Once you find the "Delete Account" option, follow the on-screen instructions to confirm your request for data deletion.

Important to Note:

  • Deleting your account will remove your personal data associated with your Helper Plan account. This may include information like your name, email address, profile picture, and any other data you have provided.
  • Once your account is deleted, you may not be able to access certain features or functionalities of the Helper Plan app that require an account.

4. Opt-Out/Unsubscribe: We may use your email address to send them information and updates. If User decides to opt-in to our mailing list, they will receive emails that may include company news, updates, related product or service information, etc. If at any time the User would like to unsubscribe from receiving future emails, we include detailed unsubscribe instructions at the bottom of each email or in your user settings.
